— About the practice

Engineering discipline, interior sensibility.

Most firms hand you off between the person who calculates load-bearing walls and the person who picks your flooring. Selvam Constructions was started to close that gap — one team carries a project from soil testing and structural drawings through to the last coat of paint and the last light fitting.

We work across residential, commercial and renovation projects in and around Chennai, with every structural decision documented and every interior choice tied back to how the building actually stands.
